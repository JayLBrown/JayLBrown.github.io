About Me


At the beginning of 2018, I didn't know where my life was going. After some serious self-reflection about my passions and interests, I committed to learning everything I could about machine learning, data science, and the tech industry.

In April I started working fulltime at Capital One as a data process manager. Here I use my technical knowledge to automate a few of the tedious business processes that always lurk in large organizations. My intention is to help my colleagues by removing the repetitive tasks that eat away at our attention each workday.
